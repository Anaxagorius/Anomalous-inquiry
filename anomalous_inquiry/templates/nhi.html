👁️ Alleged Non-Human Intelligences

← Home

A documentary archive of reported and hypothesised non-human intelligent entities drawn from government records, abduction research, contactee testimonies, indigenous traditions, parapsychological literature, and theoretical frameworks. All categories represent claims rather than established fact. No ontological status is attributed to any category herein. Entries follow the principle of phenomenological neutrality: what has been reported, by whom, and in what context.

Entity Typologies

🌌 I. Extraterrestrial Biological Entities

Greys, Nordics, Reptilians, Mantids, Sirians, Arcturians, and other alleged spacefaring biological beings.

🌍 II. Ultra-Terrestrial / Intra-Terrestrial

Earth-native non-spacefaring intelligence hypotheses: Inner Earth, Agartha, Cryptoterrestrials.

🌀 III. Interdimensional / Extra-Dimensional

Phase-shifting entities, shadow beings, hitchhiker phenomenon, future human hypothesis.

⚡ IV. Plasma-Based / Energy-Based Intelligences

Ball lightning sentience hypotheses, Hessdalen lights, atmospheric light beings, electromagnetic entities.

🔮 V. Orb-Associated Intelligences

Autonomous light orbs, shape-morphing energy spheres, responsive orbs, orbs entering solid matter.

🤖 VI. Artificial / Synthetic NHI

Biomechanical entities, Von Neumann probes, AI-controlled craft intelligences, hybrid biological-synthetic entities.

🧬 VII. Hybrid & Engineered Beings

Human-NHI hybrids, genetic experimentation products, programmed biological entities.

🏛️ VIII. Ancient / Mythological (Reinterpreted)

Anunnaki, Watchers, Nephilim, Vedic Devas, Djinn, Greek gods, Mesoamerican sky beings as NHI hypothesis.

🧠 IX. Consciousness-Based / Non-Physical Entities

Pure consciousness intelligences, disembodied minds, collective hive minds, non-local awareness entities.

🃏 X. Control System / Trickster Class

Vallée's control system hypothesis, Keel's ultraterrestrial, deceptive phenomenon entities, reality-modulating intelligence.

🌊 XI. Aquatic / Transmedium NHIs

USO-associated entities, non-biological underwater intelligences, amphibious NHIs, oceanic constructors.

❓ XII. Other Alleged Categories

Time-traveling NHIs, simulation overseers, post-biological civilisations, multi-species federations.
