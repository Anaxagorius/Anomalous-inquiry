🌟 Altairians

Beings associated with Altair — the brightest star in the constellation Aquila (the Eagle), 16.7 light-years from Earth — appear across multiple independent streams of contact literature, channeled material, and alleged insider testimony. Though less prominent in the mainstream contact narrative than Greys or Nordics, the Altairians occupy a significant position in several cosmological frameworks as members of a broader galactic confederation and as beings with a particularly ancient relationship to avian-lineage NHI species.

Stellar Context

Altair (Alpha Aquilae) is a rapidly rotating A-type main-sequence star with an equatorial rotational velocity of approximately 286 km/s — so fast that it is significantly oblate, bulging noticeably at the equator. It is among the nearest naked-eye stars and has been known since antiquity; it forms the southern vertex of the Summer Triangle asterism alongside Vega and Deneb. At 16.7 light-years, it falls within the category of near-neighbor star systems frequently cited in contact literature as close enough for relatively regular interaction with Earth.

Altair has not been confirmed to host planets with current observation technology, though its rapid rotation makes planetary system formation less likely via standard models — a fact noted by some researchers as irrelevant given claims of beings operating from alternative dimensional states.

In the constellation mythology, Aquila the Eagle was the bird of Zeus/Jupiter — the carrier of divine lightning bolts. Some researchers note the universal association of the eagle with divine messengers across cultures (Aztec, Native American, Egyptian, Greek, Norse) as potentially meaningful in the context of Altairian contact claims.

Physical Description

Altairian descriptions in the contact and channeled literature are less uniform than the more frequently described races, with two primary morphological types reported:

Humanoid Type

  • Height: 5.5–6.5 ft; proportioned similarly to humans.
  • Skin: Described variably as pale blue-white, pale gold, or light olive depending on the source — suggesting either multiple sub-types or source-dependent variation in perception/description.
  • Eyes: Almond-shaped, larger than human proportion; pale blue or golden-amber in color; several accounts note a distinctive slight upward tilt.
  • Hair: White-blonde to silver; fine. Some accounts describe no hair, only a smooth cranium.
  • Features: Fine-boned facial structure; slightly elongated skull in some accounts, though not to the degree of Greys. Overall impression is of extreme fineness or delicacy combined with an aura of presence and authority.

Avian-Hybrid Type

Several independent sources describe an Altairian sub-type or related lineage with avian characteristics — light feathering on the arms and shoulders, a faintly beak-like nose structure, and eyes with the horizontal pupil orientation of birds of prey rather than the round pupils of humans. This description is consistent with the constellation association (Aquila — the Eagle) and connects the Altairian profile to the broader Avian NHI category found in the full literature. Whether this represents a separate avian race co-inhabiting the Altair system or a distinct lineage within Altairian civilization is not clarified across sources.

Behavioral Profile & Reported Role

Across the contact literature, Altairians are described in consistently positive terms with a profile distinct from the hierarchical, procedure-focused Greys:

  • Observation and record-keeping: Multiple sources describe Altairians as long-term observers of Earth's development — maintaining detailed records of human history, biology, and consciousness evolution. This role is described as essentially archival or scholarly rather than interventionist.
  • Diplomatic function: In several channeled frameworks, Altairians serve a diplomatic or mediating role within the Galactic Federation or Council structures — potentially related to their position as a relatively neutral, non-polarized civilization in galactic political contexts.
  • Energetic healing: A subset of Altairian contact accounts includes healing encounters, particularly involving light-based or frequency-based technologies applied to the human energy body — a capability described independently by multiple channeled sources.
  • Communication style: Primarily telepathic; several accounts describe a quality of communication more precise and less emotionally colored than Pleiadian or Sirian contact — as if the information is transmitted as pure structured data rather than felt resonance.

Key Sources & Contact Cases

Alex Collier — Andromedan Framework

Contactee Alex Collier, who claims contact with beings from the Andromeda constellation since childhood, describes an extensive galactic political structure in which Altairians are member civilization. In Collier's framework (derived from his contacts with beings named Moraney and Vissaeus), Altair is described as hosting a humanoid civilization that has maintained ongoing interest in Earth's development for thousands of years. The Collier material, while focused primarily on Andromedans, provides the most detailed cosmological placement of Altairians within a galactic hierarchy of any contactee source.

Lyssa Royal — Galactic Heritage Material

Channel Lyssa Royal, through her work with the Germane entity and other sources, places Altair in the broader galactic family of humanoid civilizations connected through the Lyran ancestral lineage. Royal's The Prism of Lyra (1992) provides a cosmological framework in which Altairian beings emerged from the same Lyran ancestral stock as Pleiadians, Sirians, and Andromedans — each civilization specializing in different aspects of consciousness development.

Robert Shapiro — Superchannel Material

Robert Shapiro's extensive channeled material through Light Technology Publishing includes transmissions attributed to Altairian beings, describing their civilization in detail. Shapiro's Altairian transmissions describe a society based on consensus consciousness — individual identity maintained within a collective awareness that allows instantaneous information sharing without the hive-mind quality attributed to Greys.

Russian Alien Races Book

The Russian Alien Races Book does not contain a specific "Altairian" entry under that name in its most commonly circulated version, though several entries in the handbook describe beings from nearby systems with characteristics overlapping the Altairian description. Some researchers argue that the Altairian type may be subsumed under the handbook's broader humanoid classification categories.

Ancient Cultural Connections

The star Altair has held specific cosmological significance in multiple ancient cultures:

  • Chinese mythology: Altair is the "Cowherd Star" (Niulang) in the Chinese Qixi legend — one of the two lovers separated by the Milky Way who meet once per year when birds form a bridge. This myth connects Altair specifically to the bird-sky-bridge motif present in many cultures.
  • Japanese tradition: Altair is Hikoboshi in the Tanabata festival — one of Japan's most beloved star festivals. The narrative mirrors the Chinese version and has been celebrated for over a thousand years.
  • Sumerian astronomy: Altair appeared in Babylonian star catalogues as a significant navigation and agricultural marking star, included in the MUL.APIN astronomical compendium (~1200 BCE).
  • Native American traditions: The Eagle star (Altair) features in several Plains Indian astronomical traditions, where it represents either a messenger between worlds or a guardian of the sky roads.

Ancient astronaut researchers note these consistent bird/eagle associations across disconnected cultures and propose them as evidence of a common ancient encounter with Altairian beings whose visual appearance carried avian characteristics.
