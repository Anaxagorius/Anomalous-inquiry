🌌 Andromedans

Beings said to originate from the Andromeda galaxy or constellation — most specifically from the Zenetae star system within the Andromeda region — are documented almost exclusively through the testimony of contactee Alex Collier, whose claimed ongoing contact since childhood produced an unusually detailed and internally consistent body of cosmological information. Collier's Andromedan material, first publicly presented in 1994, covers galactic history, species hierarchies, Earth's situation from a broader galactic perspective, and specific predictions about human development — some of which Collier's supporters argue have been borne out by subsequent events.

Physical Description

Based on Alex Collier's detailed descriptions from his Andromedan contacts:

  • Height: Typically 5–6 ft, humanoid proportions.
  • Skin tone: Described as varying from blue-grey to pale blue to nearly white depending on the sub-group, with the most commonly encountered type having a slight blue tint to otherwise pale skin.
  • Eyes: Larger than human eyes in proportion, typically described as deep blue or blue-grey with a quality of depth and intelligence Collier describes as difficult to convey in words.
  • Build: Delicate but not fragile — Collier describes them as deceptively strong despite a slender appearance.
  • Lifespan: Reported as extraordinary — individual Andromedans described as hundreds of thousands of years old for the most senior beings Collier has interacted with. Age apparently brings no physical deterioration — their physical form remains stable across vast time-spans.
  • Dimensional state: Described as operating sixth and higher dimensional consciousness — as far beyond fifth-dimensional Arcturians as Arcturians are beyond third-dimensional humanity.
  • Communication: Telepathic; Collier describes receiving information as complete conceptual packages rather than sequential words or sentences.

Alex Collier — Contact History

Alex Collier (a pseudonym, used for personal safety reasons in his early public career) claims initial contact beginning in childhood in Michigan in the mid-1960s. He describes encounters with two primary Andromedan contacts:

  • Morenae: Described as approximately 400 years old, a historian and what Collier describes as his primary teacher — providing the historical and cosmological information Collier has shared publicly.
  • Vissaeus: Described as approximately 100,000 years old; rarely communicates directly but has occasionally provided information of a different quality from Morenae — deeper, more cosmological.

Collier's contact intensified in the late 1980s and early 1990s. He gave his first major public presentation in 1994 — a three-hour lecture that was filmed and has been widely circulated online, described by many researchers as one of the most detailed and thought-provoking single contactee presentations available. Subsequent interviews (Project Camelot, 2009) and regular webinars post-2010 have expanded and updated his public testimony.

Key Information from the Andromedans

The most significant cosmological information provided by Collier's Andromedan contacts, as described across his presentations and publications:

  • Human genetic composite: Earth humanity's DNA is described as a composite of 22 different extraterrestrial races' genetic material — making humans genetically unique in the galaxy and, ironically, among the most genetically rich species despite being one of the youngest.
  • Galactic age structure: Humanity is positioned as thirteenth density in a twenty-second density universe; highly young and inexperienced by galactic standards.
  • Moon and Mars: The Andromedans provided information suggesting the Moon is an artificial body, not of natural origin, and that Mars was inhabited by a humanoid civilization — connected to an ancient catastrophe — within the past 750,000 years.
  • The Andromedan Council: A governance body described as being concerned about Earth's trajectory — specifically about the potential of Earth's negative timeline to affect other civilizations across the galaxy and beyond, given humanity's unique genetic position.
  • Alpha Draconian presence: The Andromedans provided Collier with extensive information about the Alpha Draconian civilization's ancient and ongoing influence on Earth — describing them as having been in contact with Earth power structures for millennia.
  • Holographic universe: The physical universe described as a holographic construct of consciousness; physical reality as one layer of a multi-density existence.

Connections to Other Testimony

Researchers noting convergences between Collier's Andromedan material and independent sources:

  • Collier's description of 22-race human genetic composite precedes similar claims from other contactees (notably Sheldan Nidle and channeled sources) by several years.
  • His Draconian hierarchical structure overlaps with independently gathered regression cases from David Jacobs.
  • The Andromedan Council concept parallels the "Galactic Federation" described by multiple channeled sources operating independently of Collier's contact stream.
  • Collier's 1994 prediction of a significant government disclosure event and changing geopolitics has been cited by supporters as partially fulfilled by subsequent UAP disclosure events, though the timeline specifics he provided were not precise.
