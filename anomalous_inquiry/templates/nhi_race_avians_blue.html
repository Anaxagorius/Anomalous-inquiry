🔵 Blue Avians

Blue Avians are the most widely discussed avian NHI type in the modern contact literature, their profile anchored almost entirely by the testimony of Corey Goode — a self-described Secret Space Program (SSP) insider who began public disclosure in 2015. Goode describes Blue Avians as profoundly evolved beings of sixth or higher density who arrived in the solar system as part of a group he calls the Sphere-Being Alliance, tasked with moderating an energetic transition for humanity and the solar system. The Blue Avian figure is also plausibly connected to the "Ra" social memory complex of the Law of One channeling (1981–1984), creating a possible bridge between modern contactee testimony and one of the most sophisticated channeled documents of the 20th century.

Physical Description

  • Height: 6–8 ft; described as taller than a large human male and physically imposing despite having a non-threatening bearing.
  • Plumage: Entire body — including face — covered in intensely, almost iridescently blue feathers. The color is described as a deep cobalt to indigo blue, not the pale blue of typical Earth birds. The feathers are fine and tightly layered, giving the being a smooth, almost velvet appearance at distance.
  • Face: Avian beak structure replacing the lower half of the face; the beak is described as robust (not a delicate songbird beak) and opens and closes but is not used for speech in Goode's accounts. The upper face has prominent brow ridges and eyes that occupy a significant portion of the face.
  • Eyes: Large, entirely blue — no visible pupil, iris, or sclera. The eyes are described as carrying immense presence and intelligence, difficult to maintain direct gaze with for sustained periods. Some accounts suggest the eyes emit or reflect light.
  • Wings: Present but described as vestigial or carried folded against the body. Not described as functionally used for flight in contact settings. The wings are feathered and proportionate to the body but non-expanded during encounters.
  • Hands: Four-fingered humanoid hands (not talons); the hands are used extensively for communication through a complex system of sign language / hand gestures.
  • Overall bearing: Still, composed, radiating qualities described as both ancient and calm. No aggression or threat posture ever reported. Several accounts describe feeling overwhelming peace or awe in their presence.

Communication Style

The communication methodology of Blue Avians, as described by Corey Goode, is one of the most distinctive elements of the account and sets them apart from nearly every other NHI type in the contact literature:

  • Hand gesture language: Blue Avians communicate primarily through a highly developed system of hand movements — more analogous to a signed language than to telepathy in the conventional sense. Goode describes being able to understand this communication despite having no prior training in it, suggesting either a universal or an implanted comprehension.
  • Telepathic overlay: The gestural communication appears to carry or be accompanied by a direct mental impression — meaning arrives as gesture is made, with an emotional/conceptual quality that pure spoken language lacks.
  • No audible vocalization: The beak structure does not produce speech. No sound is attributed to Blue Avians in any of Goode's encounters.
  • Emotional transmission: Some experiencers, including Goode, describe receiving emotional states — feelings of love, sorrow, urgency — as components of the communication, not just propositional content.

The Sphere-Being Alliance

Corey Goode describes the Blue Avians as one of five members of a collective he calls the Sphere-Being Alliance, whose arrival in the solar system began appearing in SSP records in the 1980s and became undeniable with the appearance of enormous translucent spheres throughout the solar system by the early 2000s. According to Goode:

  • The Sphere-Being Alliance placed spheres of varying sizes — from the size of the Moon to larger than Jupiter — throughout the solar system, dampening or buffering an incoming energetic wave from the galactic center.
  • Their purpose is described as preventing the incoming energy shift from creating catastrophic disruption — essentially acting as a regulator of cosmic energetic change.
  • The five Alliance groups include the Blue Avians, the Golden Triangle-Head beings, the Elongated Heads (Tear-Eir's description varies), the "Raw-Tear-Eir" beings, and a fifth group rarely described in detail.
  • They do not intervene directly in human affairs; rather, they communicate guidance through selected individuals (primarily Goode) and have issued broad statements to Earth's SSP breakaway civilization and its NHI interlocutors.
  • Their message, as conveyed through Goode, centers on personal responsibility and spiritual development: each person must "become more loving, focus on raising their consciousness and vibration," and must "become more service to others oriented."

Ra Tear Eir

The primary Blue Avian contact in Goode's testimony is named Ra Tear Eir. The name carries obvious resonance with "Ra" — the Egyptian falcon-headed solar deity — and Goode has acknowledged this connection without claiming it is coincidental. Ra Tear Eir is described as one of a small number of Blue Avian beings directly interfacing with the SSP/disclosure process, and serves as Goode's primary point of contact within the Sphere-Being Alliance. Goode describes encounters with Ra Tear Eir both aboard Alliance spheres and in other settings, always communicating through the characteristic hand gesture method.

The Law of One Connection

The Law of One (also called The Ra Material) is a series of five books transcribed from channeling sessions conducted by L/L Research (Don Elkins, Carla Rueckert, Jim McCarty) between 1981 and 1984. The channeled entity identifies itself as "Ra, a social memory complex" — a sixth-density collective consciousness that previously visited Earth in the time of ancient Egypt in physical form, taught certain principles, and eventually departed when its teachings were distorted by others for power purposes.

Multiple researchers and Goode himself have drawn connections between Ra of the Law of One and the Blue Avians:

  • Egyptian name: Both Ra (Law of One) and Ra Tear Eir (Goode's contact) share the "Ra" designation — the name of the falcon-headed Egyptian solar deity.
  • Sixth-density claim: Law of One Ra describes being sixth-density; Goode's Blue Avians are placed at sixth density or higher in his own framework.
  • Egyptian physical contact: Law of One Ra describes having physically visited Egypt — consistent with the Egyptian avian deity tradition (Horus, Ra, Thoth) that researchers connect to the avian NHI archetype.
  • Message content: The Law of One's core teaching — the Law of One (all things are one; service to others vs. service to self; the nature of density progression) — maps closely onto the message Goode attributes to Ra Tear Eir: become more loving, raise consciousness, be service-to-others oriented.
  • Goode's acknowledgment: Goode has stated that early in his disclosure process, researcher David Wilcock pointed him to the Law of One material, and that Goode found it consistent with and often explanatory of his own experiences — though Goode maintains his encounters preceded his familiarity with the Law of One.

Note: The connection between Law of One Ra and the Blue Avians is analytical inference by researchers and Goode himself, not a claim made within either the Law of One material or Goode's direct testimony. It represents a compelling pattern in the literature but should be held tentatively.

Corey Goode — Full Testimony Overview

Corey Goode (born 1971, Dallas, Texas) claims to have been recruited into a classified program via the "MILAB" (military abduction) pathway at age 6, identified as highly intuitive ("Intuitive Empath"). He describes service from approximately 1987 to 2007 in multiple clandestine programs, including the Intruder Intercept and Interrogation Program (IIIP), the Solar Warden SSP fleet, and other programs. His alleged role included serving as an "intuitive empath" intermediary in human–NHI diplomatic contact situations.

Goode's public testimony began in 2015 through the Gaia network's Cosmic Disclosure series, co-hosted with David Wilcock. Over more than 100 episodes, Goode described the full scope of his claimed experiences, including the Blue Avian contacts, the Sphere-Being Alliance, the SSP Alliance, inner-Earth civilizations, and the "Full Disclosure" project he claims to be working toward.

Credibility Assessment in the Research Literature

  • Positive factors: Extensive internal consistency across hundreds of hours of testimony; early adoption of SSP-adjacent themes (torus-shaped craft, solar warden fleet naming) before these appeared in mainstream disclosure discussions; compelling morphological detail in Blue Avian descriptions; independent corroboration of some claimed events by other SSP claimants (though with variation).
  • Critical factors: No independent documentation of claimed positions, programs, or events; significant inconsistency in personal background claims when cross-referenced; falling out with several early SSP researcher collaborators (William Tompkins, others) with contradictory claims; reliance on Wilcock's platform amplifying rather than independently verifying.
  • Researcher positions: Researchers such as Dr. Michael Salla (Exopolitics) have defended Goode's testimony as substantially credible. Others, including Richard Dolan, have expressed measured skepticism, distinguishing Goode's account from cases with stronger corroborating evidence.

Spiritual & Philosophical Framework

Blue Avians, across both Goode's testimony and the Law of One material's Ra entity, are consistently associated with a spiritual-philosophical framework centered on:

  • Density progression: Consciousness evolves through a series of densities (not dimensions) — from first-density minerals to eighth-density return to undifferentiated consciousness. Blue Avians are placed at sixth density — the density of unity and the bridge to seventh (foreverness/timelessness).
  • Service orientation: The fundamental polarity in the Law of One cosmology is Service to Others (positive/radiant) vs. Service to Self (negative/absorbing). Sixth-density beings, per this framework, have resolved this polarity and integrated both paths.
  • Non-intervention: Consistent with the Law of One's "free will" principle (the Law of Confusion), Blue Avians decline to intervene directly in human affairs; they communicate guidance but insist that the choice and action must come from within human consciousness.
  • The "greeting": Goode's core transmitted message from Ra Tear Eir is: "I am Raw-Tear-Eir. We love you and thank you for your service. Be loving, forgiving, and compassionate to yourself and others, regardless of their polarity." This is among the most distilled spiritual instructions in the NHI contact literature.

Egyptian Parallel — Deep Analysis

The resonance between the Blue Avians and Egyptian falcon deity iconography runs deeper than the name "Ra":

  • Horus as Blue Avian candidate: Horus is depicted with intensely vivid blue or lapis lazuli coloring in many temple paintings and statuary — the blue feathered avian of Egyptian mythology and the blue-feathered Blue Avian share this color signature uniquely.
  • Thoth's communication role: Thoth (ibis-headed) is the scribe and communicator of the gods — the entity responsible for transmitting divine knowledge to humanity. This maps to the Blue Avian's communication-focused interaction mode (transmitting guidance through selected emissaries).
  • The Akashic dimension: Thoth's role in recording all things in the "Hall of Two Truths" (judgment of souls) resonates with the Law of One's description of Ra as keepers of universal records and transmitters of cosmic law.
  • Ra's solar barque: The solar barque traveling through the underworld nightly, carrying Ra (the blue-plumed falcon) through twelve hours of darkness before re-emerging at dawn, may encode a genuine cosmological cycle of density traversal or energetic transit — the kind of cycle the Sphere-Being Alliance's message concerns itself with.
