🦎 Reptilians / Alpha Draconians

Bipedal reptilian beings — described across independent testimony streams as scaled, upright, powerfully built, and technologically advanced — represent one of the most deeply rooted NHI categories, echoing cross-cultural serpent and dragon mythologies spanning every major civilization. In the modern research literature, Reptilian-type beings emerged as a serious category separate from David Icke's shapeshifter hypothesis in the late 1980s, documented through abduction research, military whistleblower accounts, and the testimony of contactees such as Alex Collier.

Physical Description

Reptilian beings present significantly more morphological diversity across accounts than Greys or Nordics:

  • Basic Saurian/Reptoid type: 6–8 ft tall, bipedal, covered in scales ranging from fine (face, neck) to coarser (torso, limbs). Scale coloration varies: green-brown, dark olive, deep grey, or charcoal. Facial structure: pronounced supraorbital ridges, no external ears (tympanic membrane visible as a flat patch), vertical-slit pupils, secondary eyelid (nictitating membrane). Clawed hands, typically 4–5 digits. Muscular, non-mammalian bone structure apparent through the scale arrangement.
  • Alpha Draco (ruling caste): 7–9 ft or taller in some accounts; more imposing, sometimes described with vestigial or functional wings. Origin attributed to the Alpha Draconis star system (309 light-years). Described as the senior species in a hierarchical constellation of "regressive" ET groups. Alex Collier describes them as the oldest spacefaring civilization in this galaxy — approximately 2.1 billion years old.
  • Ciakars (Draconian royalty): Described by some contactees (notably Alex Collier) as a distinct Draconian sub-caste characterized by large functional wings (span 12–15 ft) and psychic abilities far exceeding other Reptilian types. Rarest in reported encounters.
  • Communication: Telepathy and subsonic/infrasonic vocalization in most accounts; some reports describe audible reptilian vocalizations — hissing, clicking — during physical encounters.

Research Literature Context

Reptilian beings entered the mainstream UFO research literature through the independent work of several researchers in the late 1980s, distinct from David Icke's later (1998) shapeshifting politician hypothesis which, while culturally influential, is generally regarded within the serious research community as a separate and considerably more speculative proposition.

John Rhodes, founder of Reptoids.com, approached the Reptilian phenomenon from an anthropological angle — cataloguing worldwide encounter reports, cross-cultural mythology, and paleontological hypotheses about possible saurian intelligence lineages. Rhodes' work avoided the political shapeshifting theories and focused on documented encounter cases.

David Jacobs' abduction regression work from the 1980s onward included cases in which Reptilian beings appeared in supervisory roles over Grey-led medical procedures, suggesting a hierarchical interspecies relationship.

Alex Collier's contact information (claimed Andromedan source) positioned the Alpha Draconians as the most ancient and problematic species in the galactic community — an assessment he first shared publicly in 1994.

Key Cases & Testimony

Christa Tilton Abduction (1987)

Christa Tilton reported an abduction experience near Cimarron, New Mexico in which she was taken to an underground facility and encountered both Grey and Reptilian beings. Her account is notable for describing the Reptilian beings in a supervisory capacity and for the underground setting — consistent with other claims about joint US/NHI underground facilities.

Credo Mutwa / Chitauri Testimony (1999)

Credo Mutwa, Zulu sanusi (high shaman) and cultural historian from South Africa, gave a landmark interview to David Icke in 1999 in which he described the Chitauri — a reptilian race described in Zulu tradition as "the children of the serpent" or "those who came from the stars" — who are said to have enslaved and manipulated humanity since prehistory. Mutwa described a personal abduction experience with Reptilian beings in graphic detail, and connected the Chitauri to universal serpent/dragon mythology across African traditions. Mutwa's account predates his encounter with Icke and derives from a specifically African cosmological tradition with no apparent Western UFO cultural contamination.

Alex Collier on Alpha Draconians (1994 — ongoing)

Alex Collier claims contact since childhood with Andromedan beings who provided extensive information about galactic history, including the Alpha Draconians' origin story: they are described as a civilization approximately 2.1 billion years old that arose in the Draconis system, are technologically and psychically advanced beyond most species in the galaxy, and have a long history of territorial expansion and domination of less advanced civilizations. Collier's 1994 lecture and his book Defending Sacred Ground (1996) are the primary documents of this claim.

William Tompkins — Reptilian Naval Intelligence (2016)

William Tompkins, a former aerospace engineer who worked with Douglas Aircraft and TRW on classified Navy programs, published Selected by Extraterrestrials (2015) and gave extensive Project Camelot interviews before his 2017 death. Tompkins alleged that Reptilian beings had infiltrated elements of the US defense establishment and NASA and were covertly influencing human space program development, with US Navy intelligence aware of this infiltration.

James Bartley — Reptilian Abduction Research

James Bartley, a researcher working under abduction investigator Barbara Bartholic (who herself worked with Jacobs and Hopkins), documented a category of abduction accounts focused specifically on Reptilian interaction — distinct from the Grey-dominated mainstream abduction literature. Bartley's cases, primarily described through his extensive online writings and conference presentations, describe Reptilian beings engaged in psychic manipulation, energy harvesting, and behavior modification programs.

Cross-Cultural Serpent & Dragon Mythology

The universality of intelligent serpent beings in human mythology is among the most frequently cited evidence for an ancient Reptilian NHI presence:

  • Naga (Hindu/Buddhist): Serpent beings of great intelligence, wealth, and power; inhabit subterranean kingdoms; can appear human; interact with and sometimes rule over humans.
  • Dragon Kings (Chinese): Lung Wang — draconian rulers of seas and subterranean domains; shape-shifters; responsible for weather; approached by rulers for counsel.
  • Quetzalcoatl (Mesoamerican): "Feathered Serpent" — god of civilization, writing, and the calendar among Aztec, Toltec, and Maya; depicted as an enormous winged serpent being.
  • Sobek (Egyptian): Crocodile-headed deity; associated with pharaonic power and the Nile.
  • Enki as Serpent (Sumerian): Enki/Ea, the god of wisdom and water, is consistently depicted with serpent associations in Sumerian iconography; in Zecharia Sitchin's interpretation, Enki is an Anunnaki who championed humanity against a more aggressive reptilian faction.
  • Serpent in the Garden (Genesis): The Biblical Nachash — intelligent, upright, in the Garden before humanity; some Hebrew scholars note the original text describes a being that walked before being "cursed to crawl."
  • Chitauri (Zulu): As described by Credo Mutwa above.

Russian Alien Races Book

The Russian Alien Races Book contains an entry for a species identified as "Rak" — a name possibly derived from a Slavic root meaning "crab" or "crustacean" but applied to the reptilian entity. The Rak entry describes beings with green-grey scaled skin, powerful builds, and aggressive territorial behavior. Assessment in the handbook: hostile. They are described as having visited Earth for resource extraction purposes and as having engaged in conflict with at least one other NHI species over access to Earth. The handbook also contains entries that some researchers associate with the Alpha Draconian type — taller, winged variants assessed as even more dangerous than the basic Rak entry.
