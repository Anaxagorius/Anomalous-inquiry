🧬 NHI Species & Race Index

This index compiles documented and alleged Non-Human Intelligence (NHI) species drawn from the full breadth of the research literature: structured hypnotic regression archives, abduction case files, contactee testimony, declassified government documents, whistleblower accounts, channeled material, and the alleged Russian Alien Races Book (also known as the "Book of Alien Races" — a document claimed to be a Soviet/KGB intelligence handbook cataloguing 58 known alien species, translated and published in English by Dante Santori and Petro Kichkaev).

Each species profile presents physical descriptions, behavioral characteristics, key documented cases with dates and investigators, cross-cultural and mythological parallels, the Russian Alien Races Book entry where applicable, and a comprehensive media and resources bibliography. Phenomenological neutrality is maintained throughout: claims are presented as claims, sources are cited, and scholarly critique is noted where relevant.

Species & Race Profiles

👽 Greys (Zeta Reticulans)

Most commonly reported NHI type; small grey-skinned beings with large black eyes, multiple sub-types from different star systems.

🤍 Tall Whites

Chalky-white beings documented extensively by ex-USAF weatherman Charles Hall at Nellis AFB, 1965–67.

🌟 Nordics / Pleiadians

Tall, blonde, human-like beings; dominant in the 1950s contactee movement. Associated with the Pleiades and Lyra.

🦎 Reptilians / Alpha Draconians

Bipedal scaled beings; multiple sub-types including Alpha Draconians, Ciakars, and Saurian types. Widely paralleled in world mythology.

🦗 Mantids / Insectoids

Praying mantis-like beings, typically observed in supervisory roles during abduction encounters; associated with ancient wisdom.

🦅 Avians / Bird-Like Beings

Feathered or bird-featured entities; includes Blue Avians from Corey Goode's testimony and deep historical parallels in ancient Egypt and Hindu myth.

⚠️ Maitre

Tall, pale beings documented in the Russian Alien Races Book as among the most dangerous species known; associated with hostile interactions.

⭐ Sirians

Beings from the Sirius system; connected to ancient Egyptian cosmology, the Dogon astronomical mystery, and channeled Sirian High Council material.

💙 Arcturians

Beings from the Arcturus system; described as among the most spiritually advanced civilizations in channeled and contactee literature.

🌌 Andromedans

Beings from the Andromeda constellation; primary source is contactee Alex Collier, who delivered extensive cosmological and historical information.

🌠 Lyrans / Laan

Ancient humanoid race from Lyra; considered progenitors of most humanoid species. Documented in the Russian Alien Races Book as "Laan."

🌊 Tau Cetians

Beings from Tau Ceti (11.9 ly); the first SETI target (1960), confirmed planetary system, reportedly the most human-appearing NHI type.

🔭 EBENs (Project Serpo)

Exchange program beings from Zeta Reticuli; central to the alleged Project Serpo — a claimed US/ET personnel exchange from 1965–1978.

📡 Ummites (Planet Ummo)

Beings from Wolf 424 who communicated through thousands of pages of letters to Spanish researchers from 1965 onward; unique in the contact literature.

🌑 Shadow Beings / Hat Man

Dark, featureless entities — including the Hat Man — universally reported across cultures; among the most widely encountered anomalous beings.

🏛️ Anunnaki

Ancient Sumerian sky beings; at the center of the ancient astronaut hypothesis advanced by Zecharia Sitchin and documented in thousands of cuneiform tablets.

🛸 Egarot

Russian Alien Races Book species; brown-skinned, robust humanoids assessed as protective toward Earth. Connected to South American contact cases.

💫 Solipsi Rai

Russian Alien Races Book species; small, blue-grey beings with large heads, assessed as benevolent observers — distinct from Greys in key ways.

The Russian Alien Races Book

The Book of Alien Races (also referred to as the "Alien Races Book," "Soviet Alien Races Book," or "KGB Alien Handbook") is an alleged Soviet intelligence document that claims to catalogue 58 known extraterrestrial species. The document is said to have originated within Soviet intelligence services during the Cold War, compiled from classified contact reports, recovered vehicle analyses, and intelligence gathered through surveillance and interrogation. It was purportedly translated from Russian and published in English by Dante Santori and Petro Kichkaev, with the English edition appearing around 2013.

Each entry in the handbook includes a physical description, origin star system, assessed disposition toward humanity (hostile/neutral/benevolent), and known interactions with Earth civilizations. The provenance and authenticity of the document remain contested. No independent verification of its Soviet intelligence origins has been established, and it is presented in the research community as a significant piece of contact and whistleblower literature regardless of its ultimate origins.

Species in this index that appear in the Russian Alien Races Book include: Greys (multiple sub-types), Tall Whites (Boötes origin matching entry), Nordics / Laan (Lyran origin), Reptilians ("Rak" entry — assessed hostile), Maitre (explicitly named, assessed most dangerous), Sirians, Ummites (Wolf 424 region), Egarot, and Solipsi Rai.

This index presents documented research, reported phenomena, contactee testimony, and whistleblower claims without endorsement of any specific narrative. Species profiles cite primary sources and note scholarly critique where applicable. The Russian Alien Races Book is presented as significant contact literature; its provenance remains unverified. No conclusions about the ultimate nature or reality of described entities are made herein.
