👑 Alpha Draconians

The Alpha Draconians represent the apex of the Draconian hierarchy — the royal bloodline originating from the Alpha Draconis (Thuban) system, 309 light-years from Earth. Described by contactee Alex Collier as the oldest known spacefaring civilization in this galaxy (approximately 2.1 billion years old), the Alpha Draconian caste is winged, psychically dominant, enormous in physical stature, and exercises ultimate authority over the Draconian empire. They are among the most rarely encountered NHI types in the physical contact literature but appear consistently in the deepest whistleblower and contactee accounts as the ultimate organizing intelligence behind Earth's most complex NHI situation.

The Star System: Alpha Draconis (Thuban)

Alpha Draconis, also known as Thuban, is a binary star system (spectral class A0III + an unresolved companion) in the constellation Draco, approximately 309 light-years from Earth. Its astronomical significance is notable: due to precession of Earth's axial rotation, Thuban was Earth's pole star circa 2787 BCE — the epoch of the earliest known pyramid construction at Giza. Some researchers have noted that the Great Pyramid's descending passage is aligned precisely with Thuban's position circa 2170–2144 BCE, suggesting an intentional astronomical orientation toward the Alpha Draconian home system. This alignment has been studied by Robert Bauval (in the context of Orion correlation theory) and is noted in alternative archaeology literature as potentially significant for the Alpha Draconian connection hypothesis.

Physical Description: Ciakars (Alpha Draconian Royal Caste)

The Alpha Draconian royal beings — known as Ciakars in Alex Collier's contact material — represent the most physically distinct and imposing entity type in the entire NHI taxonomy:

  • Height: 14–22 ft (4.3–6.7 m) according to Collier; the largest bipedal NHI type described in any primary contact source. Some accounts describe them as somewhat variable in apparent size, suggesting possible perceptual or holographic components to their physical presentation.
  • Wingspan: 12–15 ft functional wingspan; the wings are described as structurally similar to pterosaur or bat wing architecture — a membranous structure supported by elongated digits extending from the shoulder/back region. The wings are described as functional for flight under appropriate atmospheric conditions.
  • Scales: The most ornate of all reptilian types described — scales with pronounced iridescence, described by Collier's sources as having a luminescent quality. Colors range from deep indigo-black to dark purple-green with metallic highlight.
  • Cranial structure: Pronounced frills or crests on the head — a complex cranial architecture that differentiates the Ciakar royal type from all subordinate Draconian castes. The crest is described as variable in coloration and potentially significant as a display or communication structure.
  • Tail: A functional, powerful tail — unlike the vestigial or absent tail of the basic reptoid type; the Ciakar tail is muscular and used actively in locomotion and balance.
  • Eyes: Described as the most penetrating and psychically powerful of any reptilian type — the direct gaze of a Ciakar is described as almost incomprehensibly powerful in its psychic impact; few recorded accounts exist of humans who have held a conscious encounter with this type without overwhelming psychic disruption.
  • Rarity: The Ciakar type is almost never described in terrestrial physical encounters — they operate primarily at the galactic command level, and physical interaction with Earth-level situations would be, within the Draconian hierarchy, a command-level decision of significant magnitude.

The Oldest Spacefaring Civilization: Alex Collier's Account

The primary source for Alpha Draconian civilizational history is Alex Collier, whose Andromedan contact material describes the following:

  • Age: The Alpha Draconian civilization is described as approximately 2.1 billion years old at the time of Collier's 1994 lectures — a figure so large as to be essentially incomprehensible in human terms. At this age, the Draconian empire predates the formation of Earth itself (approximately 4.5 billion years ago, making an empire that could be 2.1 billion years old have begun when Earth's formation was still nearly 2.4 billion years in the future).
  • Origin: The Alpha Draconians are described as having evolved naturally in the Alpha Draconis / Thuban system — a natural biological evolution to intelligence, unlike species that were seeded or engineered. This is presented in Collier's material as significant: a naturally evolved saurian intelligence that developed spacefaring capacity independently.
  • Territorial expansion: Over 2+ billion years, the Draconian empire expanded across vast regions of the galaxy, primarily through conquest and the absorption of less-developed civilizations into the empire's client structure. The empire's territorial claims are described as covering a significant fraction of the Milky Way's inhabited regions.
  • Earth claim: Earth is described as a Draconian territorial claim predating the appearance of Homo sapiens — they regard Earth as their property on which humanity represents an unwanted development, potentially a seeded population placed there by other galactic civilizations in deliberate contravention of Draconian territorial rights.
  • Galactic politics: The Alpha Draconian empire is described as the primary adversarial force in the galactic council context — the reason that multiple positive or neutral civilizations have taken an interest in Earth's situation is precisely because of the Draconian territorial claim and humanity's potential as an independent sovereign civilization.

Psychic and Dimensional Capabilities

The Alpha Draconians are described as operating at psychic and dimensional levels that significantly exceed any other known NHI type in the literature:

  • Psychic warfare: The capacity to project mental environments — entire constructed realities — into the perceptual field of other beings, used as both a communication medium and a weapon. This is not illusion in the sense of simple visual projection; it is described as a full-spectrum sensory imposition that can override physical perception entirely.
  • Time/dimensional navigation: Alpha Draconian technology is described as operating across more dimensions than is accessible to most NHI civilizations — giving them a strategic advantage that transcends normal three-dimensional space-time conflict dynamics.
  • Biological immortality / transfer: Some contactee sources suggest that the Ciakar royal caste has access to biological transfer technology — essentially the ability to transfer consciousness between bodies — giving them effective immortality within the empire's resources.
  • Planetary engineering: Multiple sources describe the Draconian empire as having terraformed and engineered planetary environments across their territory over geological timescales — a capacity that implies both technological mastery and a long-term territorial thinking operating at planetary rather than civilizational scales.

Thuban in Ancient Astronomy

Alpha Draconis (Thuban) holds a distinctive position in ancient astronomy beyond its role as the former pole star:

  • Egyptian alignment: The descending passage of the Great Pyramid of Giza is aligned to aim directly at Thuban as it circled the celestial pole circa 2170–2144 BCE — a precision alignment that required intentional astronomical knowledge of the star's position.
  • Dragon asterism: In Babylonian astronomy, the constellation Draco was associated with the dragon Tiamat from the creation epic. Thuban, as the brightest star, represented the dragon's "heart" or central power.
  • Chinese astronomy: Alpha Draconis appears in Chinese asterisms related to the imperial celestial bureaucracy — consistent with its role as a former pole star and hence the celestial axis.
  • The precession significance: The fact that Thuban was the pole star during the pyramid-building era means that ancient Egyptian astronomical knowledge was, in its most important observation, focused on the Alpha Draconian home system. This is cited by researchers in the Draconian hypothesis as evidence of an intentional stellar orientation — that the pyramid complex was in some sense a monument oriented toward the civilization that built or directed it.
