🦎 Lacertians

The Lacertians are a distinct reptilian NHI type documented primarily through the controversial "Lacerta Files" — a series of alleged interview transcripts published by Swedish researcher Ole K. beginning in 1999 and 2000. Unlike the extraterrestrial Draconian hierarchy, the Lacertians describe themselves as a terrestrial species — an ancient reptilian civilization that evolved on Earth long before Homo sapiens and has maintained subterranean communities throughout recorded human history. If the account is taken at face value, the Lacertians represent not an alien contact but an encounter with Earth's original indigenous intelligence.

The Lacerta Files: Primary Source

The Lacerta Files are alleged transcripts of two interviews conducted by Swedish researcher Ole K. with a being who identified herself as "Lacerta" — a female member of an underground reptilian species. The first interview is dated December 16, 1999; the second April 23, 2000. Ole K. claims the interviews were conducted at his home and that Lacerta appeared to be an approximately humanoid female in her thirties, but revealed her non-human nature through physical features and demonstrable non-human abilities during the interview.

The documents were initially circulated in Swedish and German UFO research communities before achieving wider distribution through English translation. They are treated within the research community as significant claimed contact documents regardless of the ongoing controversy about their authenticity — the content is internally consistent and the cosmological framework described by Lacerta is not replicated in any prior published source, which some researchers regard as evidence of genuine novel contact material rather than confabulation.

Physical Description

As described by Lacerta in the interview transcripts and corroborated in various ways through encounter reports in the broader literature:

  • Height: Approximately 5–6 ft when in bipedal form; shorter than the Draconian types but comparable to average human height. The female form described by Ole K. appeared initially human until closer examination.
  • Skin/scales: Light greenish-beige to pale olive in the interview description; fine-scaled rather than the coarser scale pattern of the larger Draconian types. The scale structure is apparently smooth enough at normal viewing distances to be mistaken for human skin coloration.
  • Eyes: Slit pupils; described as the most immediately identifiable non-human feature. Eye color in the Lacerta interview: a green-gold. The nictitating membrane is present.
  • Head: Somewhat more elongated posteriorly than a human skull; the forehead described as broader and more prominent. No external ear structures — flat tympanic patches. The facial structure described as almost-human from a distance but with proportional differences that become apparent at close range.
  • Hands and digits: Five-fingered, though with longer digits than human norm and small, flattened claw-like nails. The hands are described as appearing almost human.
  • Tail: A tail is present but described as small — apparently a more vestigial structure than in the larger Draconian types; Lacerta does not describe it as functionally significant.
  • Temperature: The skin described by Ole K. as noticeably cool to the touch — consistent with ectothermic or partially ectothermic biology — though Lacerta implies her species has developed technological means of thermal regulation.

Lacertian Civilization: Key Claims

The following is a summary of the claims made by the entity Lacerta in the interview transcripts:

  • Terrestrial origin: Lacerta states emphatically that her species evolved on Earth and is not extraterrestrial. They predate Homo sapiens by approximately 65 million years — having evolved in the aftermath of the non-avian dinosaur extinction event as the surviving intelligent dinosaurid lineage.
  • Current population and location: The underground communities of Lacertian civilization are described as situated deep beneath Earth's surface — primarily beneath mountain ranges, oceanic floors, and in naturally occurring cavern systems that have been extensively expanded with technology. The total population she describes is substantially smaller than humanity's.
  • Technology: Described as advanced well beyond human capability in several key areas: plasma and field-effect technology; a form of faster-than-light travel using what Lacerta describes as electromagnetic frequency manipulation; biological modification technology; and mind-influencing technology. She describes their technology as physically different from anything humanity has developed — not electrical in the human sense.
  • Relationship with humanity: Non-interference as a general policy; the Lacertians maintain minimal contact with the human surface civilization and observe rather than intervene. This policy is described as partly out of indifference and partly because of historical conflicts with humanity that led to a deliberate separation policy.
  • External ETs vs. the Lacertians: Lacerta distinguishes sharply between her species (terrestrial) and the various extraterrestrial species that visit Earth. She describes several ET species with apparently greater knowledge of them than a surface human would have — including hostile species that have, in her account, caused significant problems for both her civilization and humanity.
  • Historical events: Lacerta describes a major war approximately 65 million years ago between two extraterrestrial civilizations that used Earth as a battleground — the resulting catastrophe (which she distinguishes from the standard asteroid-impact model of dinosaur extinction) nearly destroyed her ancestors' earlier civilization and forced its remnants underground. This is presented as the origin of the permanent subterranean lifestyle.
  • Religion and consciousness: Lacerta describes her species as having a fundamentally different relationship with consciousness — she demonstrates apparent psychic abilities during the interview (accurately reading Ole K.'s thoughts on several occasions) and describes a species-wide psychic connectivity that differs from human individual consciousness.

Corroborating Evidence & Parallel Accounts

The Lacerta Files, while the most detailed account of a specifically terrestrial underground reptilian civilization, are not unique in the broader literature:

  • Theosophical Serpent Race: Helena Blavatsky's The Secret Doctrine (1888) describes a pre-human serpent race that ruled Earth before humanity and retreated underground — documented before any modern contact literature existed.
  • Richard Shaver and the Dero: Richard Shaver's accounts, published in Amazing Stories magazine beginning in 1945, describe degenerate underground beings (the "Dero") inhabiting ancient cavern systems beneath Earth's surface — a pre-Lacerta account of subterranean non-human intelligence, though with significantly different characteristics.
  • The Inner Earth traditions: A global tradition of subterranean civilization exists across cultures — from Agarthi and Shambhala in Asian esoteric traditions to the Hollow Earth claims of Admiral Richard Byrd and others — which the Lacertian account intersects with, though the Lacertian description is more biologically grounded than the spiritualized inner-Earth traditions.
  • Native American accounts: Multiple Native American traditions describe encounters with serpentine or lizard-like beings associated with underground locations — particularly in the American Southwest (Hopi, Pueblo) where similar features to the Lacerta description appear in traditional accounts.
  • The dinosauroid hypothesis: Palaeontologist Dale Russell of the Canadian Museum of Nature published a scientific paper in 1982 (Paleobiology) proposing that, had the non-avian dinosaurs not gone extinct, the most intelligent late Cretaceous dinosaur species (Troodon) would likely have evolved along a convergent path toward bipedal intelligence — a morphology he called the "Troodontid Person" or dinosauroid. Russell's scientific model produces a creature remarkably similar to the Lacertian description.
