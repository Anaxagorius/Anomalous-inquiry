👻 Paranormal

← Home

A documentary archive of reported paranormal phenomena drawn from folklore, historical records, witness testimony, investigative case files, and cross-cultural tradition. Categories span apparitions, undead entities, shapeshifters, demonic forces, witchcraft, and haunted locations. All entries represent claims, accounts, and hypotheses rather than verified phenomena. Material is presented for research and educational purposes without endorsement of any supernatural framework.

Entity & Phenomenon Categories

🌫️ I. Ghosts & Apparitions

Residual hauntings, intelligent apparitions, poltergeists, shadow figures, and crisis apparitions across historical and contemporary reports.

🧛 II. Vampires & Blood Entities

Eastern European strigoi and vrykolakas, psychic vampirism, historical exhumation cases, and cross-cultural blood-drinking entity traditions.

🐺 III. Werewolves & Shapeshifters

Lycanthropy folklore, historical trial records, clinical lycanthropy, skin-walkers, naguals, and other human-animal transformation traditions worldwide.

🧟 IV. Zombies & Revenants

Haitian vodou zombie tradition, draugr of Norse mythology, revenant accounts from medieval chronicles, and ethnobotanical theories.

😈 V. Demons & Malevolent Entities

Demonic possession case files, exorcism records, djinn traditions, incubus and succubus reports, and cross-cultural malevolent entity typologies.

🔮 VI. Witchcraft & Cursed Objects

Historical witch trials, maleficium accounts, cursed object investigations, hex traditions, and the intersection of folk magic with documented anomalies.

🏚️ VII. Haunted Locations

Documented haunted sites: Winchester Mystery House, Eastern State Penitentiary, Gettysburg, the Tower of London, and other repeatedly investigated locations.

This archive presents reported phenomena and folklore traditions without endorsement of any supernatural or metaphysical framework. All entries represent claims derived from witness testimony, historical records, cross-cultural oral tradition, and investigative case files. No conclusions about the objective reality, mechanism, or ultimate nature of any described phenomenon are made herein.
