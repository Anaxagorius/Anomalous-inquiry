⚡ Psychokinesis

Psychokinesis (PK) refers to the claimed ability of mind to influence physical systems without known physical interaction. Research distinguishes macro-PK (large-scale, visually observable effects) from micro-PK (statistical deviations in random physical systems).

The PEAR Lab at Princeton studied operator influence on Random Event Generators (REGs) over 12 years, finding small but reportedly statistically significant deviations. Skeptics point to methodological issues and insufficient independent replication.

Poltergeist phenomena, historically categorized under "Recurrent Spontaneous Psychokinesis" (RSPK) by W.G. Roll, involve reported object movements in domestic settings, often centered on individuals under psychological stress.
