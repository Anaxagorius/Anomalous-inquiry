🛸 Unidentified Aerial Phenomena

← Home

A documentary archive of reported UAP incidents, declassified government records, physical trace evidence, and emerging scientific frameworks. Cases span military encounters, mass civilian sightings, and close encounters with physical effects. No conclusions about origin or mechanism are implied. The archive follows Hynek classification conventions and prioritises cases with corroborating witnesses, instrumentation data, or official investigation.
